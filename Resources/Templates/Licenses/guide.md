## Which License To Use?

This question depends on the software.

*What follows is my own opinionated list*

- CODE: [`BSD-3-Clause`](https://opensource.org/licenses/BSD-3-Clause)

   - Application, tool, distributable binary: [`AGPL-3.0`](https://opensource.org/licenses/AGPL-3.0)

   - "Library" or otherwise multi-module toolkit/framework/project: [`LGPL-2.0`](https://opensource.org/licenses/LGPL-2.0)

   - Actual, for real library or framework to be used by everyone: [`Apache-2.0`](https://spdx.org/licenses/Apache-2.0.html)

   -

- EXPOSITION/CREATIVE: [`CC-BY-NC-ND-4.0`](https://spdx.org/licenses/CC-BY-NC-ND-4.0.html))
